OS Contact App
Redesigning a System App

Role: Product Designer & Manager | Information Architect

Team Size: 12      Date: 2015/7-2015/1

Category: Interaction DesignMobile App Information Architecture

Intro

Thunder OS is a ROM based on android that is free to download and install through 360 PC Security software. It provides solution for low price smart phones to maintain fast speed and long battery life. Initially, it only involves one key feature which is system optimization, but as the user base grows, the company want to make it into a fully functional 3rd-party ROM. This app is the first attempt in taking over some of the system's app.

Goal

Design a system contact app that provides users with better user experience and performance.

Challenge

This is a product targeting low-end smart phones that are already slow in performance and our product should be a solution but not a burden as compared to their current system contacts app.

The product was projected to launch in a month, which includes design, development, testing, and adaptation to multiple devices.

Due to the high cost in developing from scratch, we are required to work with an existing product that was designed as a third party contact app, so our design in features was restrainted by this.

My Role

For this project, I was the sole product designer on the team, but because this app was made from an existing app, my job was focused on reorganizing the entire information architecture and deciding how to present it and through what interaction style. I also worked closely with two UI designers to ensure that the interface delivers the brand image we are trying to create.

The whole development team consisted of four android developers and six QA engineers. As the product manager, I also worked closely with them to ensure that the whole team meets the deadline given by executives.

Research

The research was mostly done by the company's marketing and user research team, which I no longer have access to. Here is an overview of what research was done and what insights was took to inform the design.

  • Competitive Analysis: MIUI, Color OS, EMUI, Android L, iOS

  • Research: 3 focus group interviews & 100+ marketing research surveys

Insights

Chinese android phone users who are interested in customized ROM are early adaptors of new things. They want an avant-garde interface that differentiates them from regular users. They are also people who work under high pressure, so an ideal contact app need to have advance features and simple interactions to ensure productivity.

Design

Main Features

Dailer, Contacts, Message, Spam-blocker, Multiple Sim Card Support

Information Architecture

Present only the most relevant information upfront, and hide all additional features into hidden menu and long press action.

Wireframe

I simplified the information structure by making all subfeatures related to each section stay in the hamburger menu on the top right of nav-bar, and hide additional actions into the long press interaction.

Interface

Thanks to our awesome UI Designer Wen & Huan, we developed an interface that adopts material design but include more viewable signifiers to avoid confusion.
