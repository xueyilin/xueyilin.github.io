Intro

  • Thunder OS is a ROM based on android that is free to download and install through 360 PC Security software.

  • It provides solution for low price smart phones to maintain fast speed and long battery life.

  • Goal: Design a system contact app that provides users with better user experience and performance.

Challenge

  • This is a product targeting low-end smart phones that are already slow in performance and our product should be a solution but not a burden as compared to their current system contacts app.

  • Due to the high cost in developing from scratch, we are required to work with an existing product that was designed as a third party contact app, so our design in features was restrainted by this.

  • The product was projected to launch in a month, which includes design, development, testing, and adaptation to multiple device modals.

Research

  • Competetive Analysis: MIUI, Color OS, EMUI, Android L, iOS

  • Research: 3 focus group interview & 100+ Marketing Research Surveys

  • Insight 1: Chinese android phone users who are interested in customized ROM are early adaptors of new things. They want an avant-garde interface that differentiates them from regular users.

  • Insight 2: They are people who work under high pressure, so an ideal contact app need to have advance features and simple interactions to ensure productivity.

Design

  • Main Features: Dailer, Contacts, Message, Spam-blocker, Multiple Sim Card Support

  • Information Architecture: Present only the most relevant information upfront, and hide all additional features into hidden menu and long press action.

  • Wireframe: Simplifying the information structure, highlighting only the important information.

  • Interface: Adapting material design but include more viewable signifiers to avoid confusion.

  • Animation: Smooth but simple animation to enhance the sense of high quality.
